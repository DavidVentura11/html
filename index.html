Destination: Sri lanka

Some facts about Sri lanka:

Sri lanka,formerly known as Ceylon and officially the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is an island country in South Asia. It lies in the Indian Ocean, southwest of the Bay of Bengal, separated from the Indian peninsula by the Gulf of Mannar and the Palk Strait. Wikipedia
Capitals: Colombo, Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte
Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee
Population: 22.16 million (2021) World Bank

Description of the trip:

On 21/6/23 I flew together with my two friends to Sri Lanka. The flight was long and included two stops. We landed on the next day (22/6) in the capital city - Colombo. We slept at "granbell hotel" for one night and then we moved to our second place that called Arugam Bay. The duration of the trip was 8 hours and along the way we saw many kinds of cool animals in the wild like elephants and tigers. In Arugam Bay we stayed at "Jetwing surf hotel" - a stunning hotel that designed in a rustic style with lots of palm trees and picturesque wooden cabins. We were in Arugam Bay for four nights and then we moved by car to the last place of our trip - Ella. Ella is a breathtaking waypoint in Sri Lanka. It has spectacular nature and a lot of picturesque and green scenery that cannot be explained. We visited cool sites like "Nine Arches Bridge , "Adam's Peak Road" and "Elephant Rock".
We stayed in a hotel that called "mountains Heavens". A hotel that is located in a very high mountainous area and from which there is a crazy view on the whole nature of these. We were there for two nights and on the last day we started our way back home and got a taxi for the way to the airport in Colombo. The drive was 6 hours but we were satisfied with everything we experienced. When we arrived at the airport we said goodbye to our friend the taxi driver who accompanied us throughout the trip and then we boarded to the plane on the way back home to Israel.
Definitly it was my best trip!
